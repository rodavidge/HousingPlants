Pay and Job Outlook

The projected growth for this career field is 8% from 2019 to 2029 which is much faster than average. This is because a lot of ecommerce is continuing to expand and online purchasing is growing faster than ever in the retail industry, according to the U.S Bureau of Labor Statistics. There were about 13,400 openings for web developrs and digital designers projected each year. Overall the job opportunities are expected to be good. Source: U.S Bureau of Labor Statistics
