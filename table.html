DIFFERENT PLANTS NEED DIFFERENT THINGS

This table will give you information on 20 different common houseplants. This will include their name, best growing conditions as well as difficulty level.
PLANT NAME GROWING CONDITIONS EXPERIENCE LEVEL
Asparagus Fern Will grow great on windowsills as long as there's plenty of water and light. Difficulty: 2/10
Chinese Evergreen It only needs moderate water and feeding. It does need many hours of indirect sunlight to help maintain its color. Difficulty:3/10
Monstera It's fast growing even in low light conditions and is hardier when it comes to watering and feeding. You should be careful to not overwater it. It also needs some extra dusting and pruning to keep it looking great. Difficulty: 3/10
Maranta Prayer Plant This one is high maintenance to keep it healthy long term. You can't overwater, forget to mist or forget to feed it or the leave tips will brown. Difficulty: 5/10
Parlor Palm It's slow growing but easy to keep alive. You have to water them carefully and keep them away from bright sunlight so the leaves don't burn. Difficulty: 4/10
Pothos It only needs moderate light, but more sun will encourage more vines and can dry out a little between waterings and will need some trimmings. Difficulty: 2/10
Fiddle Leaf Fig This one is sensitve to drafts and temperature and needs humidity to thrive as well as proper indirect light. Difficulty:7/10
Guiana Chesnut It's practically immune to overwatering and is happy in low light conditions; it's practically indestructible. Difficulty: 1/10
Chinese Money Plant This one needs monthly feed, lots of indirect light, and frequent watering to thrive. It also needs to stay moist and be rotated regularly. Difficulty:4/10
Alocasia It grows in low light but it does need high humidity (more than most homes can provide). It also needs regular dusting, feeding and pest control. Difficulty:5/10
Yucca It needs plenty of bright light and can't be overwatered. Difficulty Level:4/10
African Violets It requires constant moisture and high humidity, but it also hates having wet leaves. It also needs consistent indirect light and warmth. Difficulty:6/10
Peperomia It's slow growing, but low maintenance and recovers from negect. Only needs moderate water and bright, indirect light. Difficulty:1-3/10
Air Plant They need good air circulation, regular misting with distilled water, bright light and constant temperatures to keep them happy. Difficulty:7/10
Spider Plant They don't need a lot of water and won't be too bad if the soil dries out. Bright light is best but shouldn't be in direct sun too long. Difficulty:2/10
Birds Nest Fern It doesn't want to be fed, but it does need soil that is constantly moist but never waterlogged. Difficulty:6/10
Peace Lily You need to keep the soil moist, mist it regularly, and give it monthly feed. It prefers indirect sunlight but needs many hours of light each day for flowering. Difficulty:4/10
Aloe This one wants prolonged exposure to indirect sunlight. It can dry out between watering but give a good soaking when you do. Best in a terracotta pot with special cactus soil. Difficulty:5/10
English Ivy It prefers regular watering and dislikes direct sun and temperature needs to be consistent. Difficulty:3/10
Dragon Tree It needs a place with moderate light but not direct summer sun as well as regular watering. Difficulty:5/10
